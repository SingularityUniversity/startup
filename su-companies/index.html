The SU Startup Network

The SU Startup Network is a unique membership offering; providing startups access to carefully curated networks of domain experts, seasoned entrepreneurs and business leaders, investors, global Fortune 500 companies, and the media. Participating companies leverage exponential technologies to address humanity’s grand challenges.

Members of the SU Startup Network gain direct access to Singularity University’s faculty and experts, receive advice and mentorship from serial entrepreneurs and leaders, tap into a vast network of vetted funders, form partnerships with global Fortune 500 companies, amplify their media and PR presence, and are featured at Singularity University’s global events and programs.

Portfolio companies have raised more than $150M, are featured in leading media outlets around the world, and formed partnerships with companies such as Google, GE, Cisco, Autodesk, UNICEF, World Health Organization, Lowe’s and Swiss Cargo.
