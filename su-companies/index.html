The SU Companies Program

The SU Companies Program provides continuous support and services to significantly accelerate the growth of your business. The SU Companies Program has cultivates and supports global startups using breakthrough technologies for social impact. Many of which have the potential to disrupt or create entire industries. To date SU Companies have raised more than $100 million in combined funding and are providing goods and services globally.

SU Companies frequently make the news and have presented at Google’s exclusive “Solve for [x]” forum, TEDGlobal, and SXSW and have been featured in publications including Wired Magazine, Bloomberg TV, VentureBeat, BBC News, Al Jazeera America, The Science Channel, and many more.

To join the SU Companies Program you must either be a SU alumni or be referred by an SU community member.

SU Companies receive an “Unfair Advantage”

1. Corporate Membership Program and Fortune 500 Network

2. Investor Network

Invitation to SU investor events and programs plus facilitated investor introductions to support your fundraising.

3. Participation at SU Events

Opportunity to participate in SU Programs, Conferences, and Summits, both locally and internationally to increase the reach of your network.

Access to an incredible pool of mentors, advisors, and faculty.

Referrals and discounts with relevant partners and services, from legal to web-hosting.

6. PR/Media

Inclusion in Singularity Hub, SU press, outreach, and social media.

7. Space

Work from our coworking facility on the SU Campus. Work alongside founders, entrepreneurs in residence, and Corporate Innovation partners.
