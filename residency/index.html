The SU Labs E.I.R. Program

Our Entrepreneur in Residence program is a unique opportunity for entrepreneurs and change-makers, who work at the intersection of technology and global impact, to work alongside the SU Labs community.

The E.I.R. Experience

Work out of our SU Labs office in the heart of Silicon Valley, collaborate with SU Companies, exchange ideas with our Corporate Members, immerse yourself in the SU Community and participate in many other activities at Singularity University.

The SU Labs E.I.R. program is from one to three months long and open to entrepreneurs from all around the world. We provide the program at no cost – all we ask for is that you immerse yourself in the community and positively impact those you come in touch with.

Participation

To participate in our E.I.R. program we are looking for entrepreneurs who have done interesting work at the intersection of technology and global impact.

To apply for the program please fill out the short application form and we’ll be in touch shortly.
