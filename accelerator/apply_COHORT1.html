Startup Accelerator

Solving humanity's grand challenges by leveraging technology.

Apply to the SU Startup Lab Accelerator

Applications for the first accelerator program built specifically for startups tackling humanity’s grand challenges leveraging exponential technologies are now open.

Application Process

  • STEP ONE – Fill out the application

    Provide us with some basic information about you and your company as well as the product or service you're working on.

  • STEP TWO – Interview

    We’ll do our best to review applications on a weekly basis and select strong applicants to join us for a 30-minute interview asap.

Requirements

The SU Startup Accelerator supports early-stage entrepreneurs leveraging exponential technology at scale to address one of humanity's grand challenges.

Our target ventures are existing teams of at least 2 co-founders with skill sets covering technology, business, design, prototyping and impact/social entrepreneurship. They are pursuing a sustainable, scalable, for- or non-profit business strategy and have built an early stage prototype of their product or service.

One to two co-founders must work full-time out of the SU LABS space in Mountain View, CA for the full 10 weeks of the program and attend the kick-off bootcamp and retreat, weekly speaker series and share-outs, and monthly design reviews. Please note that it is your responsibility to make sure you can participate in our program in person; we can not help you with any visa-related questions or issues.

We provide dedicated support services, community, space, and funding to our accelerator companies. Our investment stake will depend on the stage and structure of your company and will be in the typical accelerator range. For non-profit organizations we provide funding in the form of an unrestricted grant.

Timeline

Our current target is to launch the inaugural class of the Startup Accelerator on March 23rd, 2015. We will close applications on January 23rd, 2015 and let the selected teams know about their participation on February 14th, 2015. However, the timing is subject to change. Our focus is assembling the best, most complementary cohort of start-ups, providing value to all applicants throughout the process through establishing early relationships, and launching the cohort once a solid class forms.
