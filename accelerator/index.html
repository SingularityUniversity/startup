The SU Labs Startup Accelerator

Welcome to the first accelerator program built specifically for startups (both for- and non-profit) tackling humanity’s grand challenges leveraging exponential technology.

The Startup Experience

In an intense hands-on 10-week experience on the Singularity University campus in the heart of Silicon Valley we will take startups from prototype or early proof-of-concept to scale. Starting with a one-week Bootcamp experience, the SU Labs Startup Accelerator team will guide you through four two-week long sprints, each focused on a different area of your business.

Our unique global educational position provides fertile grounds for startups to connect with leaders on business, leadership, product, and marketing development. We combine the best that SU has to offer by presenting startups to unparalleled access to an invested community made of Fortune 1000 companies in our overbooked Corporate Executive Program, thought leaders enrolled in our Google-sponsored Graduate Studies Program, alumni in our international network hubs, and accomplished attendees of the X-Prize/SU/Deloitte joint effort, the Innovation Partnership Program.

» Read more about the program structure on our blog.

We provide $100,000 in seed funding to give you runway throughout the program, a co-working space at NASA Research Park, a structured program offering and the best Singularity University has to offer for 7-10% equity in your company. For non-profits/501(c)(3) organizations we provide $50,000 in unrestricted grants. All with the goal to help you build products positively impacting humanity at scale.
